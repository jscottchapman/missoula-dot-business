- [ ] File DBA for "Missoula.business" with Montana Secretary of State

## Setup Steps
1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
2. Click "Add your business"
3. Business name: **Missoula.business** (must match your DBA filing)
4. Primary category: **"Business management consultant"**
5. Additional categories: "Computer consultant," "Internet marketing service," "Software company," "Information technology company"
6. "Do you want to add a location customers can visit?" → **No** (service-area business)
7. Service areas: Missoula, MT / Missoula County / Lolo / Florence / Frenchtown / East Missoula / Bonner
8. Phone: (406) 200-8107
9. Website: https://missoula.business
10. Use your home address (required internally, NOT shown publicly)

## Google Voice Warning
- Free Google Voice is designated for **personal use only**
- May fail phone/SMS verification — you'll likely get video verification instead
- FCC 10DLC regulations (2025+) increase compliance risk with free tier
- **Safest option:** Google Workspace ($10/mo) gives business-grade Voice
