# Direct Mail Campaign Plan

## Why Direct Mail
- Response rate: 4.4% average (37x higher than email)
- In a small market where you're unknown, physical mail from a local 406 number has more trust than a Facebook ad
- Postcards sit on desks for days; ads get scrolled past in 0.5 seconds

## Budget: ~$300 for 500 Postcards

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Design | $0 (Canva or self-made) |
| Printing (500 oversized 6"x9") | $50-$75 |
| Postage (USPS Marketing Mail) | $185-$205 |
| Mailing list (targeted local businesses) | $23 |
| **Total** | **~$300** |

## Expected Results
- 4% response rate = **20 responses** from 500 mailers
- 25% close rate = **5 clients**
- At $500 average first project = **$2,500 revenue**
- **ROI: ~8x**

## Key Principles
- Drive to the existing lead magnet (SEO audit) — the funnel is already built
- The postcard's job is NOT to sell — it's to get them to the website
- Include phone number prominently — Montana business owners prefer to call
- DO NOT lead with "AI" — lead with pain/outcome
- Zero-risk offer
