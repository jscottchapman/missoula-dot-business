# Revenue Plan — missoula.business

## Productized Services (Sell Today)

### Tier 1: Door-Openers ($300-$750)

| Package | Price | Your Time | Margin | Pitch Angle |
|---------|-------|-----------|--------|-------------|
| SEO Quick Fix | $300 | 2-3 hrs | ~90% | "Your audit found 5 issues. I'll fix them." |
| Google Review Auto-Responder | $500 | 2-3 hrs | ~85% | "Every review gets a thoughtful response, automatically." |
| Lead Follow-Up Automator | $500 | 3-4 hrs | ~85% | "Every inquiry gets a response in under 60 seconds." |
| Social Media Content Pipeline | $750 | 4-5 hrs | ~80% | "AI drafts all your posts. You approve in 30 min/week." |

### Tier 2: Custom Automations ($1,500-$3,000)

| Package | Price | Your Time |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| Operations Autopilot (intake→schedule→follow-up→invoice) | $1,500-$2,500 | 10-15 hrs |
| AI Customer Service Agent (chatbot/SMS responder) | $2,000-$3,000 | 15-20 hrs |

### Tier 3: Full Builds ($5,000-$10,000+)
- Custom AI platforms (Salud Circle-type work)
- Only pursue after cash flow from Tiers 1-2

### Pricing Rules
- NEVER charge hourly — fixed packages only
- Missoula is NOT SF/NYC — $500 is a significant investment for most local businesses
- Anything above $5K needs quantified ROI ("saves you $X/month")
- Every setup ends with: "Want me to manage this ongoing for $X/month?"

## Recurring Revenue (MRR)

| Retainer Service | Monthly Price | Your Time/Month |
|-----------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Social Media Management + AI Content | $500-$800 | 2-4 hrs |
| AI Systems Maintenance ("care plan") | $200-$400 | ~0 (on-call) |
| Review Management + Reputation | $300-$500 | 1-2 hrs |
| Lead Nurture Management | $400-$600 | 2-3 hrs |

### The Math
- **$5K/month:** 10 clients at $500/month
- **$10K/month:** 15-20 retainers OR 10 retainers + 2 custom builds/month
- $10K requires expanding beyond Missoula (Bozeman, Helena, remote)

### Retention
- Monthly reports showing measurable results
- Quarterly 15-min optimization calls
- Annual discount (12 months → month 13 free)

## 3 Verticals to Own in Missoula

1. **Restaurants/bars/breweries** — disproportionate number, you know hospitality (Salud Circle), review + social = easy sell
2. **Professional services** (dentists, lawyers, accountants, realtors) — have money, recurring needs, hate marketing
3. **Outdoor recreation/tourism** (guides, outfitters, lodges) — seasonal, desperately need automation in busy season

## Revenue Timeline

| Period | Target | Focus |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Month 1-3 | $1,000-$3,000/mo | Chamber, direct mail, cold outreach, first 3-5 clients |
| Month 4-6 | $3,000-$6,000/mo | Convert setups to retainers, referral partnerships, local media |
| Month 7-12 | $6,000-$12,000/mo | Raise prices 20-30%, expand geographically, consider subcontractor |

## Time Allocation (First 3 Months)
- **40% selling** (networking, cold outreach, follow-ups, walking into businesses)
- **30% delivering** (client work)
- **20% building pipeline** (content, workshops, direct mail)
- **10% improving systems** (templates, automations, website)

---

## What NOT to Do
1. Don't build another tool — you have enough. SELL.
2. Don't say "AI" to non-technical people — say "automatic" or describe the outcome
3. Don't run paid ads until you have 2-3 case studies
4. Don't charge hourly
5. Don't teach at UM for income (adjunct = ~$200/week)
6. Don't list 9 services — pick 3 packages
7. Don't custom-quote every project — fixed prices
