## Referral Program
- Offer existing clients **$200 credit** (or cash) for every referral that becomes a paying client
- In a small town, one happy restaurant owner tells two more. Those tell four more.

## Cold Email (Legal, Effective)
- **Legal under CAN-SPAM:** B2B cold email is legal in the US without prior consent
- **Requirements:** Physical address, opt-out mechanism, honest subject line
- **Volume:** 5-10 personalized emails per day, NOT mass blasts
- **Personalization:** "I noticed [Business Name] has 47 Google reviews but hasn't responded to the last 12..."
- **Source:** Chamber Business Directory, Google Maps by category
- **Offer:** "I ran a quick audit on your website and found 3 things. Want the report?"

## Complementary Business Partnerships
- **Accountants/bookkeepers** — trusted advisors, don't compete with you. Referral fee: $200/converted client
- **Marketing agencies/freelancers** — they design, you automate. Be their technical arm.
- **Web designers** — clients always ask "now what?" after launch. You're the answer.
- **Commercial insurance agents** — know every business in town. Same referral structure.
