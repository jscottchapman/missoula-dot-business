## Email 3 — Day 14

**Subject:** 3 things you can fix today (and 1 thing you probably can't)

Hi {{first_name}},

Here are three things you can do this week to immediately improve your online presence:

**1. Claim your Google Business Profile** (business.google.com)
Fill out every field — hours, services, description, photos. Businesses with complete profiles are 2.7x more likely to be considered reputable. This is free and takes 20 minutes.

**2. Add an FAQ section to your website**
Write 5-10 questions your customers actually ask. This helps Google AND AI assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.) surface your business as an answer. This alone can dramatically improve your visibility.

**3. Ask your last 5 happy customers for Google reviews**
Send them a direct link. Reviews are the #1 local ranking factor. A simple text message works.

**Here's the one thing you probably can't do yourself:**
